About Us

Our history

High Risk Pregnancy Doctors is Maternal-Fetal-Medicine Hospitalist Group a leading provider in Obstetrical Hospital Services.
Founded in 2022, it is the only independent national provider of MFM subspecialists.
We are a group of highly trained experts to care for patients with complex maternal and fetal conditions throughout their labor and delivery journey. We provide patient care exclusively in a hospital setting.
Through its obstetric hospitalist program consisting of a Maternal-Fetal-Medicine (MFM) Physicians on site 24/7, the Company improves the standard of care for expectant mothers.
We serve as a resource and final decision-making step in patient care to our OB/GYN colleagues. By having in-depth knowledge of complex Maternal and Fetal conditions, we provide management that prevents unnecessary transfer to a Higher Level of Care.
Our focus and expertise is MFM OB hospitalist medicine, which uniquely allows our group to improve clinical outcomes, quality and reduce medical risk.
We are the leaders in outsourced high and low-risk obstetrical services.
